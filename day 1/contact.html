Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please contact our office with your insurance information, and we'll verify your coverage and benefits.

How long are sessions?

Initial assessments typically last 60-90 minutes. Follow-up therapy sessions are usually 45-50 minutes, though this can vary based on your child's needs.

Can parents attend sessions?

Yes! Parent involvement is crucial, especially for younger children. We encourage family participation and provide parent training as part of our comprehensive approach.

What should I bring to the first appointment?

Please bring your insurance card, completed intake forms (we'll send these before your appointment), any previous evaluation reports, and a list of current medications if applicable.
