Privacy Policy

1. Is my child’s information kept confidential?

Yes. All information shared with us is treated as confidential. We respect the privacy of both children and parents and handle information with care.

2. Do you collect personal information from children?

No. We do not knowingly collect personal information directly from children. Any information is provided by parents or legal guardians with consent.

3. How is my information used?

Information is used only to respond to inquiries, provide guidance, and improve our services. We do not use data for advertising or marketing.

4. Is the AI chatbot safe to use?

Yes. The chatbot provides general guidance only. It does not store personal conversations or provide medical diagnoses.

5. Do you share information with third parties?

No. We do not sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties, except when required by law.

6. Are online interactions private?

Yes. Online interactions are conducted in a secure environment designed to protect privacy and confidentiality.

7. Can I request deletion of my data?

Yes. Parents or guardians may request deletion of their information at any time by contacting us.

8. How do you protect stored data?

We use appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect data from unauthorized access or misuse.

9. Does this platform provide medical diagnosis?

No. This platform provides general psychological guidance only and does not replace professional diagnosis or therapy.

10. What should I do in an emergency?

This platform is not intended for emergencies. If your child is in immediate danger, please contact local emergency services or a licensed mental health professional immediately.

We are committed to creating a safe, respectful, and confidential environment for children and families.
